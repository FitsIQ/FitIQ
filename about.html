About FitIQ

We’re on a mission to make personal styling accessible to everyone through intuitive, AI-powered fashion intelligence.

Our story

FitIQ was born from a simple observation: finding clothes that truly fit and flatter shouldn’t be a luxury reserved for people who can afford personal stylists. Millions of people struggle with understanding their body shape, picking colors that suit them, and building a wardrobe that feels like “them”.

Founded in 2025, FitIQ brings together fashion experts, AI researchers, and product builders who believe that confidence in how you look changes how you show up in the world. We set out to pack the experience of a top-tier stylist into an app that lives in your pocket.

By combining advanced computer vision with practical styling frameworks, FitIQ delivers guidance that’s both intelligent and deeply human: what works for your body, your taste, and your real life.

Our mission

To empower everyone to understand their unique style, feel confident in their appearance, and make smarter wardrobe decisions through accessible AI.

We believe that understanding your body shape, skin tone, and personal style shouldn’t require expensive consultations or years of fashion education. Our technology makes this knowledge instantly available to anyone with a smartphone.

By blending the intuition of stylists with data from real wardrobes and real bodies, we’re working toward a fashion world that’s more inclusive, sustainable, and personal.

Our values

The principles that guide every product decision and interaction.

Inclusivity

Fashion is for every body. We design FitIQ for a wide range of shapes, sizes, genders, and identities, and we continually work to reduce bias in our models.

Innovation

We push what’s possible with AI, but always in service of clarity and confidence for the person using the app.

Privacy

Your photos and data are personal. We treat them that way, with strong security, clear controls, and no surprise selling of your information.

Empowerment

We don’t just tell you what to wear—we help you understand why something works so you can build your own style intuition.

Sustainability

By helping you buy less but better and get more from what you already own, we support a more responsible fashion ecosystem.

Excellence

From our analysis engine to our UI, we hold a high bar for craft and continuously improve based on real user feedback.
